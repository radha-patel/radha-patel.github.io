Spot(ify) The Diff

MIT WebLab

Spot(ify) the Diff is a music social networking site that analyzes you and your friends' music history to provide custom listening insights. Spot(ify) the Diff generates a compatibility score quantifying the similarities between you and your friends' music tastes as well as playlists customized for you and a friend. Finally, Spot(ify) the Diff highlights you and your friends' top artists and top tracks.

My team and I created this website using React, Node.js, Express.js, and MongoDB. You can check it our at https://spot-the-diff.herokuapp.com/.
